About

My Journey

of Curiosity and Growth

In the year 2020, amidst the abundance of free time brought upon by the Covid-19 pandemic, I found myself captivated by the world of computers. Initially, my days were filled with endless TikTok video marathons, but fate had something different in store for me. It was through the amusing mockery of the time-consuming updating process on TikTok that I discovered Linux, a powerful operating system.

Intrigued by what I saw, I decided to embark on a new adventure and delve into the realm of Linux. Armed with a USB drive and fueled by curiosity, I sought out the most user-friendly Linux distribution available for beginners. Little did I know that this simple act would ignite a lifelong passion within me.

From that point forward, my fascination with technology and computers became an all-encompassing pursuit. I immersed myself in a world of possibilities, eagerly exploring every facet of this captivating field. As I delved deeper, I encountered a variety of integrated development environments (IDEs), such as Vim, Visual Studio Code (VSCode), and many others, each offering its own unique set of tools and functionalities.

Fuelled by my burgeoning knowledge, I began constructing websites, each endeavor enhancing my understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. With every line of code I wrote, my curiosity grew, pushing me further to unravel the mysteries of web development and design.

Through my experiences, I have come to appreciate the power and potential of technology. It has transformed me from a mere spectator into an active participant in the ever-evolving digital landscape. The challenges I have faced and the skills I have acquired have shaped me into a professional ready to tackle the complexities of modern web development.

Join me on this extraordinary journey as I continue to explore and expand my skills, creating innovative and impactful digital experiences that push the boundaries of what is possible.
